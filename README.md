# 📊 Loan Default Early Risk Analytics

## 1. Project Overview
**Objective:** Identify early signals of loan default risk to help financial institutions reduce losses and improve lending decisions.  
**Tools Used:**  
- SQL (Business Queries)  
- Power BI (Interactive Dashboard)  
- Business Problem Documentation  

---

## 2. Business Problem
Loan defaults reduce profitability and increase non‑performing assets (NPAs). Detecting risk early enables banks to:
- Adjust lending policies  
- Improve credit risk scoring  
- Reduce exposure to high‑risk borrowers  

---

## 3. Data & Queries
The `Business Queries.sql` file contains SQL scripts to answer key business questions:
- Loan approval vs. rejection trends  
- Default rates by borrower segment (income, loan type, tenure)  
- Early warning indicators (delayed payments, high debt‑to‑income ratios)  

---

### Dashboard Features:

## 5. Methodology
1. **Data Preparation** – Handle missing values, normalize categorical fields.  
2. **SQL Analysis** – Extract insights on borrower behavior and loan performance.  
3. **Visualization** – Build Power BI dashboard for stakeholder decision‑making.  
4. **Business Insights** – Translate analytics into actionable recommendations.  

---

## 6. Key Insights
- Higher default risk among **low‑income borrowers** and **long‑tenure loans**.  
- **Debt‑to‑Income ratio** is a strong predictor of default.  
- Early missed payments correlate strongly with eventual default.  

---

## 7. Stakeholder Recommendations
### 🔹 Credit Policy Adjustments
- Tighten approval criteria for high DTI borrowers.  
- Limit exposure to long‑tenure loans in risky segments.  

### 🔹 Early Warning System
- Flag borrowers with delayed first payments.  
- Monitor repayment behavior monthly via dashboards.  

### 🔹 Risk Segmentation
- Create borrower risk tiers (low, medium, high).  
- Offer differentiated interest rates and collateral requirements.  

### 🔹 Portfolio Diversification
- Reduce concentration in high‑risk loan categories.  
- Encourage secured loans over unsecured personal loans.  

---

## 8. Business Value
- **Reduced NPAs** → Stronger financial health  
- **Improved Risk Management** → Better compliance with regulatory norms  
- **Data‑Driven Decisions** → Transparent lending policies
